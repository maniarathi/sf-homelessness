  San Francisco’s ‘Sadobabies’ street kids in the 1980s


    A close look at the tightly knit subculture of homeless teens

*Sadobabies

*
/A 1988 8mm video documentary about the street children of San
Francisco. The video documents the expressive traditions…

/www.folkstreams.net
<http://www.folkstreams.net/film,89><http://www.folkstreams.net/film,89>

Shot by Nancy Kalow with an 8mm camcorder from 1986 to 1988, the
documentary /Sadobabies/ take a fascinating look at the folk culture of
homeless teens in San Francisco. The film captures the daily lives of a
closely knit group who squatted in the abandoned Polytechnic High
School. “Sadobabies” were dolls crafted by the teens on which they
mirrored the physical, sexual and mental abuse they experienced in their
families.
