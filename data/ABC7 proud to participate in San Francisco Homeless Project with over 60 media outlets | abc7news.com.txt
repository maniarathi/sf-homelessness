SAN FRANCISCO (KGO) --
This week begins a unique effort among Bay Area media organizations to
put a spotlight on one issue in San Francisco that has been persistent
for decades: The plight of the homeless.

*MORE DETAILS: Read the letter of intent to learn more about the purpose
of the project
<http://abc7news.com/society/letter-of-intent-for-sf-homeless-project/1402500/>*

<https://www.projecthomelessconnect.org/>" with more than 60 other media
outlets.
The SF Homeless project <http://abc7news.com/sf-homeless-project/> will
culminate on Wednesday with media outlets blanketing the Bay Area with
stories covering all aspects of the homeless crisis.
