On the July 1st edition of Your Call, it’s our Friday media roundtable.
This week, we’ll talk with reporters about the coverage of homelessness
in the Bay Area, and how it is reported nationwide.

More than 70 media organizations banded together to bring attention to
the growing homeless crisis and explore possible solutions. What did you
learn from the coverage? And did it raise public awareness? Join the
conversation, on the next Your Call, with Matt Martin, and you.

*Guests:*

Gale Holland, staff writer with the LA Times

Daniel Beekman, staff reporter with Seattle Times 

Kevin Fagan, longtime reporter at the San Francisco Chronicle
