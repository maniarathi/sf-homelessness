  Change of Address

A series of audio profiles by Sonic SF

What’s it like to experience housing instability? What kinds of things
are important to keep close by? How do you feel safe, find warmth and
get clean? Who is there to help you and how and where do you find peace?
Listen to these audio profiles of San Francisco residents who are either
currently — or on the verge of becoming — homeless.

/Special thanks to Lava Mae <http://lavamae.org/>, Catholic Charities of San Francisco
<http://catholiccharitiessf.org/?gclid=CPnW3uHSvs0CFUZrfgodDwACOQ>, San Francisco Public Press <http://sfpublicpress.org/> and all the folks who shared their stories with us./
