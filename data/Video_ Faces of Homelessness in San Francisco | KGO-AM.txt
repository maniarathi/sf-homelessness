  Video: Faces of Homelessness in San Francisco

Posted on June 29, 2016

Homelessness in the Bay area reflects the crisis that shadows the
American dream.

Ironically, the tens of thousands of vets, mentally ill, and families
living under bridges and in cardboard boxes have become so ubiquitous
that we no longer see them. As we rush off to our next appointment, we
avert our eyes, and eventually push them from our hearts. But they are
there, fighting for scraps, for change, for their lives.

And the question remains; if not us, who will help them?
