/ 53:00

On the June 30th edition of Your Call, we’ll continue our week-long
series on San Francisco’s homeless crisis by opening the lines to take
your questions for Jeff Kositsky.

Kositsky is the city’s new director of the Department of Homelessness
and Supportive Housing. People who are living or have lived on the
streets, service providers, and advocates say a lot more is needed to
seriously tackle this issue.

How is the city addressing this crisis? What will it take to end
homelessness? It’s Your Call with Matt Martin, and you.

*Guest*:

Jeff Kositsky, director of the Department of Homelessness and Supportive
Housing
